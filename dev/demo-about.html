DR. PRESTON BURNSLearn More About Our Dentist

Dr. G. Prston Burns III was born in Virginia and grew up in Stafford County. After graduating from Stafford Senior High School he went on to earn a Bachelor of Science in biology from James Madison University

Following in his father's footstapes, he was accepted early into the MCV Dental School at Virginia Commonwealth University in richmond. He graduated Magna Cum Laude in 1999 and returned to Fredericksburg to join the practice with his father. He received his training for intravenous concvious sedation from the Smerican society for the Advancement of Anesthesia in Dentistry at St. Joseph Medical Center in Patterson, New Jersey.

Dr. Bruns moved the practice into the digital age by introducing CAD/CAM computer assisted crowns and onlays with CEREC. He was one of the first dentists in the country to begin using Invisalign (computer genereated clear braces) and now has over 10 years experiecne with the system. Keeping the practice modern, he recently put somputers in the operatories for digital x-ray cpabilities and for future technology.

Dr. Burns has been an active member of the dental community. He has been President or Vice-President of the Rappahannock Valley Dental Society for the past 10 years. He is also an officer in the Virginia Association of Dentists for Entravenous Sedation. He gices his time to the community by volunteering hes services at the Moss Free Clinic in Fredericksburg.

Dr. Burns acquired ownership of his father's practice in Octborer, 2011 and took on the new practice name of Burn Famiy Dentistry, PC. He resides in stafford wiht his wife Sandy and their two young daughters.
